# FinTech Ecosystem & Digital Banking Models Analysis

## Overview
This project compares traditional banks, challenger banks, and neobanks,
and analyzes the broader FinTech ecosystem across payments, lending,
wealth management, insurance, and RegTech sectors.

## Banking Models Compared
- Traditional Banks: JPMorgan Chase
- Challenger Banks: Starling Bank
- Neobanks: Revolut

## FinTech Sectors Covered
- Digital Banking (Revolut vs N26)
- Payments & Transfers (PayPal vs Wise)
- Lending (LendingClub vs Funding Circle)
- Wealth Management (Betterment vs Nutmeg)
- InsurTech (Lemonade vs ZhongAn)
- RegTech (Chainalysis vs ComplyAdvantage)

## Role of Software in FinTech
- Automation & operational efficiency
- AI-driven analytics and fraud detection
- API-based integrations (Open Banking)
- Customer-centric digital experiences

## Key Insights
- Neobanks scale faster due to cloud-native infrastructure
- Regulatory complexity increases with global expansion
- Software is the primary competitive advantage in FinTech
- RegTech and AI-driven compliance are becoming critical

Business Analysis | FinTech Strategy | Banking Models | Regulatory Awareness | Data Interpretation
